Lights Up! Theatre Co. officially launched on July 9th of 2021. Lights Up! was founded by Grayson Hart, "I wanted to provide an exciting opportunity that reached every person and many aspects of theatre." Lights Up! is youth-focused and driven. "We wanted to have not only youth on stage, but also have them working backstage or on the creative team." Lights Up! wants to let everyone shine. Lights Up! Theatre Co. performed their first show in October of 2021, the entire creative team, minus one, was completely made up of students. "With our first show we really planted ourselves and saw what worked and what didn't, but what made everything show unique was allowing those students to tap into all their abilities."

Lights Up! Theatre Co. is dedicated to boosting confidence, allowing everyone to shine, and creating more art within the community. Everyone is encouraged to get involved in anyway that they feel lead. To volunteer click below to contact us!
